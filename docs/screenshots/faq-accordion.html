How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to https://mysignins.microsoft.com
  2. Click on "Security info" in the left navigation
  3. Select "Change password"
  4. Enter your current password and your new password (must be at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters)
  5. Click "Submit" to save your new password

If you are locked out and cannot access your account, contact the IT Help Desk at ext. 4357 or email helpdesk@contoso.com.

What is the VPN setup process?

To connect to the corporate VPN:

  1. Download the GlobalProtect VPN Client from the Software Center
  2. Install the client and restart your computer
  3. Open GlobalProtect and enter the portal address: vpn.contoso.com
  4. Authenticate with your corporate credentials and complete the MFA prompt
  5. Click "Connect" -- the status should show "Connected"

For detailed instructions with screenshots, see the full VPN Setup Guide.
